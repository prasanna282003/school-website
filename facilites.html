Smart Classrooms

×

Our smart classrooms are designed to make learning interactive and engaging through the use of modern technology. Each classroom is equipped with:

  • Interactive whiteboards with touch-screen capability
  • High-definition projectors and audio systems
  • Digital content aligned with curriculum
  • Internet connectivity for real-time information access
  • Comfortable and ergonomic seating arrangements
  • Proper lighting and ventilation systems

These digital classrooms help teachers present complex concepts in an easy-to-understand visual format, making learning more effective and enjoyable for students.

Science Laboratories

×

Our well-equipped science laboratories provide students with hands-on experience to complement theoretical learning. We have separate labs for physics, chemistry, and biology, each designed to meet international standards.

Physics Lab Features:

  • Modern equipment for mechanics, optics, and electricity experiments
  • Digital measurement instruments
  • Demonstration tables with power supply

Chemistry Lab Features:

  • Fume hoods and proper ventilation
  • Chemical storage with safety cabinets
  • Digital balances and modern glassware

Biology Lab Features:

  • Microscopes with digital imaging
  • Specimen collection and models
  • Dissection equipment with proper disposal

All labs are supervised by qualified instructors and maintained with strict safety protocols to ensure a secure learning environment.

Computer Laboratory

×

Our computer lab is equipped with the latest technology to prepare students for the digital world. The lab provides a platform for learning programming, digital literacy, and research skills.

  • 40+ high-performance computers with latest specifications
  • High-speed internet connectivity
  • Licensed software for programming, graphic design, and office applications
  • Interactive learning software
  • Projector and audio system for demonstrations
  • Air-conditioned environment for equipment protection
  • Networked printers and scanners

Students learn various computer applications, coding languages, and digital skills under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

Sports Facilities

×

We believe in the holistic development of students, and our sports facilities are designed to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Our campus features:

Outdoor Facilities:

  • Cricket ground with practice nets
  • Football field with proper markings
  • Basketball court with floodlights
  • Volleyball court
  • 400m running track
  • Long jump and high jump pits

Indoor Facilities:

  • Table tennis tables
  • Chess and carrom boards
  • Badminton court
  • Gymnastics equipment
  • Yoga and meditation room

We have qualified coaches for various sports and regularly participate in inter-school competitions. Annual sports day is a major event in our school calendar.

School Library

×

Our library is a treasure trove of knowledge with a vast collection of books, periodicals, and digital resources. The library provides a quiet space for study and research.

  • 15,000+ books covering various subjects
  • Reference section with encyclopedias and dictionaries
  • Fiction and non-fiction collections
  • Periodicals and magazines section
  • Digital library with e-books and online journals
  • Reading area with comfortable seating
  • Computer terminals for research
  • Photocopying facility

The library is managed by a qualified librarian who assists students in finding resources and developing research skills. We regularly update our collection to include new publications.

Transport Facilities

×

We provide safe and reliable transportation services for students with a fleet of well-maintained buses covering all major routes in the area.

  • GPS-enabled buses for real-time tracking
  • Female attendants on all buses
  • First-aid kits and emergency protocols
  • Regular maintenance and safety checks
  • Trained and experienced drivers
  • Pick-up and drop-off points near residences
  • Seat belts and child safety features

Parents can track bus locations through our school app, and we maintain strict discipline and punctuality. Transport fees are charged separately and routes are optimized each year based on student needs.
