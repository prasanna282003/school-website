Welcome to Khidwai English School

Established with a vision to provide quality education, Khidwai English School has been a beacon of learning in Shimoga for over two decades. Our mission is to develop well-rounded, confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential.

We provide a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to explore their potential and achieve their personal best in all aspects of school life. Our comprehensive curriculum and range of extracurricular activities provide the perfect balance to nurture happy, well-educated children.

We maintain an inclusive environment that acknowledges and respects children from diverse family and cultural backgrounds. Our staff is committed to providing your child with high-quality education and care in a safe, respectful and inclusive environment that builds a foundation for life-long learning.

Our Core Values

EXCELLENCE

We pursue the highest standards in academic achievement and organisational performance.

EQUITY

We foster an environment that serves all students and aspires to eliminate the achievement gap.

PURPOSE

Our purpose is to champion a new model of learning that connects the timeless lessons from nature to a relevant and effective preparation for fast-changing future.

INTEGRITY

We build positive relationships through honesty, respect and compassion, which enhance the self-esteem, safety, and well-being of our students, parents and staff.
