Our Academic Programs

Providing quality education with a curriculum designed to nurture young minds

Excellence in Education

At Khidwai English School, we offer a comprehensive academic program that fosters intellectual growth, creativity, and character development. Our curriculum is designed to meet the needs of all learners and prepare them for success in higher education and beyond.

Our Curriculum

We follow the Karnataka State Board curriculum with an emphasis on holistic development. Our program includes:

Primary School

Grades 1 - 5

  • English Language Arts
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Studies
  • Kannada/Hindi
  • Art & Craft
  • Physical Education

Middle School

Grades 6 - 8

  • English, Kannada, Hindi
  • Mathematics & Science
  • Social Studies
  • Computer Science
  • Physical Education
  • Arts & Music

High School

Grades 9 - 10

  • English, Kannada, Hindi
  • Mathematics & Science
  • Social Science
  • Computer Applications
  • Physical Education
  • Optional Subjects

Academic Features

Our school offers unique programs that enhance the learning experience:

Smart Classes

Technology-integrated classrooms with digital boards and multimedia content to make learning interactive and engaging.

Science Labs

Well-equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry and Biology to promote hands-on learning and scientific inquiry.

Library

Extensive collection of books, periodicals and digital resources to encourage reading and research skills.

Remedial Classes

Special attention for students needing extra help to ensure no child is left behind in their learning journey.

About us

We provide the highest quality education so that all of our students are empowered to lead productive and fulfilling lives as lifelong learners and responsible citizens.
